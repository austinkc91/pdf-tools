Frequently Asked Questions

Is this really free? ▼
Yes, 100% free. No sign-up, no watermarks, no file limits. All processing happens in your browser using JavaScript — we never see your files.
Are my files safe? ▼
Absolutely. Your PDF files are processed entirely on your device. Nothing is uploaded to any server. This means your sensitive documents stay private — always.
How many PDFs can I merge? ▼
As many as your browser can handle — typically dozens of files. You can drag and drop to reorder them before merging.
Does compression reduce quality? ▼
Our compression removes unnecessary metadata and optimizes internal PDF structures. Text and vector graphics remain crisp. Image quality is slightly reduced at higher compression levels but remains very readable.
Does this work on mobile? ▼
Yes! PDF Tools works on any device with a modern web browser — desktop, tablet, or phone. No app download needed.
